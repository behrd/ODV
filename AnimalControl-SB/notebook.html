Animal Care & Control Data Analysis: South Bend

Introduction

This is the first in a series of reports, attempting to show which datasets are publicly available in my hometown of South Bend, Indiana. My motivation for this project is twofold: the first objective is to stress the importance of robust open data and the second is to highlight the improvements that are necessary for South Bend’s Open Data Portal.

With that out of the way, this first report will cover the “Animal Care and Control Monthly Statistics” dataset, located here.
